Lydian de Poel

Creative Content Producer & Marcom Project Manager


Lydian de Poel is a creative content producer and project manager with more than 10 years experience in planning & organisation of on- & offline marketing materials. She translates creative ideas into projects, concepts & content. Currently occupied by transformation studying webdevelopment. A creative jack of all trades who loves to laugh.

After completing her Bachelor degree Media & Culture at the University of Amsterdam Lydian worked in marketing communication positions at some of the most well-known retail companies such as Mexx, ASICS and Action.

She developed her passion for brand design early on at Mexx where she started producing & designing communication guidelines for the European store fleet. She went on managing the window & store campaign visuals & prop designs for the European store fleet at Mexx and ASICS. At ASICS she expanded her expertise when she had the delightful opportunity to art-direct some of the creative window campaign concepts for their European store fleet of the ASICS Tiger & Onutsika Tiger sneaker brands.

It wasn’t until she got the opportunity to set up a new creative studio at Edelman Amsterdam that she really found her passion for creative content production. As Studio Manager at Edelman Lydian played a big part in the successful production of creative products & services for clients such as Shell, Princes Foods, Microsoft & Dorel Juvenile. She became an expert on integrated campaigns across emerging channels, such as TV, print, digital and social.
